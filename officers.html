Founding of St. John’s Lodge

The first officers and early brethren.

The lodge history records that brethren met in late 1842 to petition for dispensation, with the first installation taking place on January 20, 1843. The early record identifies Brice McNeeley Sr. as Master, William Rea as Senior Warden, and James Robinson as Junior Warden.

  • First Installation: January 20, 1843
  • First Master: Brice McNeeley Sr.
  • Senior Warden: William Rea
  • Junior Warden: James Robinson
  • First Initiated Member: James Bell
  • Earlier 1822 Petition Names: Brice McNeeley, William Rea, James Robinson, John Moore, Peter Carmichael, James Neeley, Andrew Paul, John McIntyre
